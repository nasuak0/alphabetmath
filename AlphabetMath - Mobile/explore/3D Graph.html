Pythagoras, twice. On the floor, the dashed diagonal squares to a² + b². Now stand that right triangle up: the floor diagonal and the height c make a second right angle, so the space diagonal squares to a² + b² + c². Same lemma, one more dimension — drag the graph to see both triangles at once.
